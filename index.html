Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name for the security deposit, and in most cases, you must be at least 21 years old (some car categories may have higher age requirements).

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Yes, all our rentals include basic insurance coverage. You can choose to upgrade to more comprehensive coverage at the counter for additional protection.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time through our website or mobile app.

What happens if I return the car late?

We allow a 29-minute grace period. After that, late fees will apply. If you know you'll be late, please contact us as soon as possible to discuss options.

Can I rent a car one-way?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between most of our locations. Additional fees may apply depending on the drop-off location.
