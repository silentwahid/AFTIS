Who We Are

AFTIS was founded with a vision to redefine luxury car rentals in Dubai, combining world-class vehicles with exceptional service.

From Humble Beginnings to Market Leaders

Founded in 2015, AFTIS began as a small fleet of premium vehicles serving Dubai's elite business community. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail quickly set us apart in the competitive luxury rental market.

Today, we boast one of the most exclusive collections of high-performance and luxury vehicles in the UAE, serving discerning clients from around the world.

What began as a passion project between car enthusiasts has grown into Dubai's most trusted name for premium automotive experiences.
